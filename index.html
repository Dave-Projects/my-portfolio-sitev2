DIY Coin Slot Integration with Arduino

DIY Coin Slot Integration with Arduino is a hands-on tutorial series I created to help hobbyists, students, and aspiring tech innovators set up a working coin slot module using Arduino. The idea came from the need for simple, direct, and beginner-friendly Filipino content that teaches electronics and embedded programming in Taglish.

As of now, it covers topics such as connecting a coin slot module to an Arduino, reading pulse signals from coins and bills, setting up interrupts for accurate counting, and displaying total inserted amounts. In the near future, more videos about expanding this setup—like integrating displays, relays, and wireless monitoring will be uploaded.

My channel has a little over 5 subscribers, with over 2,500 views and counting. All of my videos can be viewed for free.

In addition to that, I have a deep admiration for classical music, especially the compositions of J.S. Bach and Beethoven. I actually play the Guitar, Piano, Drums and violin myself, and my goal is to become skilled enough to perform some of their intricate and breathtaking pieces. Recently, I completed my interpretation of Chopin's Op. 9 No. 2, and my current focus is on Buencamino's Damdamin for solo piano.
